EL-BADMUS TRAVELS - LAGOS, NIGERIA

Things to See and Do:

  • Other beaches: Visit Oniru Private Beach, a beach and pool club
  • Art galleries: Check out the Nike Centre For Art And Culture, Art Twenty One,
    Henrimoweta African Art Centre, Kokopelli, Red Door Art Gallery, or Alexis Galleries
  • Shopping: Go to The Lennox Mall
  • Historic sites: Visit Freedom Park Lagos
  • Flea and street markets: Go to Lekki Market
  • Game and entertainment centers: Try Leisure Sports Paintball Landmark or
    Crusifix Games and Lounge
  • Shooting ranges: Go to Seal Team Paintball
  • Gift and specialty shops: Visit House of Deola
  • Paint and pottery studios: Try The Metaphor
  • Concerts: Go to New Afrika Shrine
  • Bars and clubs: Visit The Soul Lounge
  • Spas: Go to Apples And Oranges Total Body Therapy
